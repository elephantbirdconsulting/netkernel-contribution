KBO

The Crossroads Bank for Enterprises (BCE/KBO) is a register containing comprehensive identification data related to businesses and their ‘establishment units’ (i.e. business locations). It includes data from the national register of legal entities, the trade register and VAT and NSSO information. More info: KBO

KBO as Open Data

Starting from May 9th 2014, the KBO data will become available as Open Data: data which can be freely reused.

KBO as Linked Open Data

This site aims to be a showcase how Open Data can be published according to the Linked Open Data publishing principles and best practices.
